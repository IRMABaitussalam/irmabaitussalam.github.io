About Us: An Extracurricular at SMA Negeri 22 Garut Engaged in Religious Activities

SMA Negeri 22 Garut is a senior high school located in the middle of Cisompet District, Garut Regency, West Java. It has been a place for various extracurricular activities aimed at developing students' talents and interests. One prominent extracurricular in this school is Ikatan Remaja Masjid (IRMA) Baitussalam, a platform for students to strengthen their religious life.


Introduction

Ikatan Remaja Masjid (IRMA) Baitussalam is an organization established with the aim of nurturing and developing religious activities among teenagers. This extracurricular plays a vital role in bridging the students of SMA Negeri 22 Garut with religious values and strengthening their connection with the local mosques in their surrounding community.


While being a part of IRMA Baitussalam, students of SMA Negeri 22 Garut have the opportunity to expand their social circle with peers who share the same interest in enhancing their religious life. They can share knowledge, experiences, and inspiration to strengthen their religious beliefs.

Main Focus

One of the main focuses of IRMA Baitussalam is to provide a deep understanding of Islamic teachings to students. In order to achieve this goal, the extracurricular organizes various activities such as religious study sessions, Quranic studies, religious discussions, and social welfare activities. IRMA Baitussalam also conducts religious education programs designed to enhance students' understanding of Islamic values and akhlak mahmudah (virtuous behavior).


In an effort to expand its positive influence, IRMA Baitussalam also collaborates with external institutions and communities beyond SMA Negeri 22 Garut. They engage in joint activities with other religious organizations, attend seminars and workshops featuring experienced speakers, participate in religious-related competitions, or initiate larger-scale religious events, such as consultations with the management IRMA Kabupaten Garut and IRMA Jawa Barat.

  • What can I Gain by Joining IRMA Baitussalam?

    By joining IRMA Baitussalam, you will receive valuable benefits and experiences that can assist you in personal development and religious life. Here are some of the things you can gain by joining IRMA Baitussalam:

    3. Developing Leadership Skills and Social Awareness: IRMA Baitussalam provides leadership training and fosters social awareness among its members. You will learn effective communication, teamwork, and leadership skills. This will help you face challenges and become a responsible leader in various aspects of life.

    4. Organizational Experience: Joining IRMA Baitussalam will provide you with valuable organizational experience. You will learn how to manage activities, work in teams, and develop managerial skills that can be useful in your career and everyday life.

    5. Broadening Social Connections: Joining IRMA Baitussalam also provides you with an opportunity to expand your network of friendships and acquaintances. You will meet people with similar interests who can support and inspire each other.
